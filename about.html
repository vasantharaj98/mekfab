ABOUT

Mekelamani Fabrications is an ISO 9001:2015 company involved in manufacturing carbon activated rotary kiln, Pressure vessels, Heat exchangers, Butterfly valve bodies, Earth moving equipment components, Robot risers, Storage Tanks, Electricity tanks Process Equipments , Static & Rotary Equipments as per ASME, ASTM, DIN, IS, TEMA, API 610 standards. We are an IBR (Indian Boilers Regulation) approved facility. We have great experience in exporting fabricated components to several countries including USA and SriLanka. We are manufacturing quality products confirming to various International codes like ASME, IS, TEMA, BS under the inspection of inspection agencies like IBR, IRS, NPOL and TUV.

Mekelamani Fabrications was founded in the year 1984 by Mr Namashivayam, who has more than 50 years of experience in the field of fabrication. We are located at 235-D Thadagam road, somayampalayam (PO),Coimbatore-641108, just about 19 kms from the Coimbatore International Airport. We are strategically located at Coimbatore, the industrial hub of South India and just hours away from the Cochin port, well suited for export shipments. We also have a marketing facility in the United States to oversee our export operations.

Mekelamani Fabrications has a team of qualified and experienced Engineers at all levels, thriving to produce best quality products at competitive price, along with on-time delivery.
